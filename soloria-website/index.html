About

At Soloria Rice, our mission is to deliver high-quality Nigerian rice that families can trust and enjoy every day. We believe rice is more than just a staple food — it is the heart of many Nigerian meals and a symbol of togetherness. Our goal is to provide clean, well-processed, and great-tasting rice that brings families together around the table and supports local agriculture with pride.

We are committed to sustainability and ethical sourcing, ensuring that our products are not only delicious but also responsibly produced. By supporting local farmers and producers, we aim to contribute to a healthier planet and a more vibrant food culture.
